About

This is a simple financial portal, created as part of my final project at the Summer 2015 Coder Foundry Masters Bootcamp in Charlotte, North Carolina.

It allows you to enter financial information for bank accounts, credit cards, cash laying around, etc. You decide how you want to organize the information; and if you like, and it lets you reconcile your records with the bank statements.

You can also set monthly budgets and see how you're doing compared to those budgets. It's not much, but it works (well, at least that's the plan!).

Enjoy!
